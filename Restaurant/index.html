About Us

Welcome to Tanjra, where we bring the rich flavors of Mediterranean cuisine to your table. Our restaurant is dedicated to providing an unforgettable dining experience with a menu that celebrates the diverse and vibrant culinary traditions of the Mediterranean region.

From our carefully crafted dishes to our warm and inviting atmosphere, we strive to create a place where you can savor the essence of Mediterranean culture and hospitality.
